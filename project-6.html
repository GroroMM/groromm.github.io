绽放的细胞

Micro

衍纸海报设计展现微观之美
Quilling poster design shows the beauty of micro world

项目背景/Background

这个项目的灵感来自于我的专业——生物工程。 当我做生物实验的时候，我发现透过显微镜看到的微观世界是如此美丽，充满活力和创造力。 由此我希望通过这种方式来唤起人们，更关注微观、关注细节。有所成就也要从小事做起。

This project was inspired by my background, bioengineering. When I did biological experiments, I found that the micro world through the microscope was so beautiful, with full of vitality and creativity. Based on that I hope to evoke people, focusing more on the micro world and paying attention to the details. To achieve something great， we need start from the little things.

解决方案/Solution

Micro 是一种衍纸工艺和海报设计。它展示了三种植物细胞的微结构和细节：草本双子叶植物茎、罂粟籽头和圣约翰草

Micro is a quilling craft and poster design. It showed the micro structure and details of three plant cells: Herbaceous dicot stem, Poppy seed head and St. John’s wort
