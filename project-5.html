空气净化岗亭

Airlet

使警卫在雾霾天执勤时也能获得净化空气
Enable the entrance guard to get purified air on the smoggy day

痛点/Problem

面对日益严重的雾霾，我们认为每个人都应该有机会在雾霾天保护自己。 警卫长时间在室外工作且着装要求不允许带口罩， 如何才能在不损害警卫职业形象、不违反着装规定的情况下，保护其免受工作场所雾霾的侵扰，同时协助他们开展工作呢？

Facing the increasingly serious smog, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to protect themselves from smog. Entrance guards are required to work outdoors for a long time due to their job attributes. How might we protect the entrance guard from the smog in their working places, without blemishing the professional image and following the dress code, at the same time assist their work?

解决方案/Solution

AIRLET 是一款专为警卫设计的岗亭，旨在净化雾霾天的空气，同时设计了可供警卫和访客操作的交互大屏，极大提升其工作效率。

AIRLET is a booth designed specifically for entrance guards. It aims to purify the air on smoggy days. It also has a large interactive screen that can be operated by entrance guards and visitors, greatly improving the work efficiency.

项目时间/Duration

2018.10 ~ 2018.12
